Title: Nakamarra
Artist: Hiatus Kaiyote
Genre: Neo-Soul / Jazz-Funk / Future Soul
Key: Eb Major (modal shifts and color chords throughout)
Tempo: Mid-slow groove
Time Signature: 4/4

[Intro]
Ebmaj7 – Dbmaj7 (x2)
Ebmaj7 – Dbmaj7 – Ebmaj7 (x2)

Notes:
- Lush major 7th chords set a dreamy, floating harmonic bed
- Alternating between Ebmaj7 and Dbmaj7 evokes suspended emotional state
- Modal interchange and stepwise descent (Eb → Db) build gentle tension

[Verse 1]

Fm7 – Cmaj7 – Abmaj7 – Ab6
Ebmaj7 – Abmaj7 – Fm7 – Cmaj7 – Abmaj7

Notes:
- Progression mixes major and minor 7ths with added 6ths for smoothness
- Cmaj7 adds unexpected brightness mid-phrase, modally ambiguous
- Root motion supports the lyrical imagery of emotional connection and space

[Refrain / Chorus]
Ebmaj7 – Dbmaj7 (repeated)
"I love you, I love you, I love you I do"

Notes:
- Repeated two-chord vamp becomes a harmonic mantra
- Very little movement — emotionally hypnotic
- Dbmaj7 is not diatonically part of Eb major, creating gentle contrast
